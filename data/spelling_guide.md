# Tibeb Authoritative Amharic Spelling Guide
# These are the ONLY accepted spellings in all Tibeb training data.
# Last updated: 2026-02-28

## Greetings & Common Words
- ሰላም (not ሠላም)
- ሰው (not ሠው)
- ሀገር (not ሃገር or ሐገር)
- አስተዳደር (not ዓስተዳደር)

## Address Forms — CRITICAL
- እርስዎ (formal/elders — not እርሶ)
- አንተ (masculine informal)
- አንቺ (feminine informal)
- አቶ (Mr.)
- ወይዘሮ (Mrs.)
- ወይዘሪት (Miss)

## Financial Terms
- ኢንቨስትመንት (investment)
- አክሲዮን (share/stock — not አክስዮን)
- ቲ-ቢል (T-Bill — hyphenated, not ቲቢል)
- ወለድ (interest)
- ትርፍ (profit)
- ኪሳራ (loss)
- ብር (Birr — not ብርር)
- ምንዛሬ (exchange rate — not ምንዛሪ)
- ቁጠባ (savings)
- ኢንቨስት (invest)
- ፋይናንስ (finance)
- ገበያ (market)
- ዋጋ (price)
- ጨረታ (auction)
- ወለድ ተመን (interest rate)
- ምርት ገበያ (commodity exchange)

## Institutions
- ኢትዮጵያ ሴኩሪቲስ ኤክስቼንጅ (Ethiopian Securities Exchange)
- ብሔራዊ ባንክ (National Bank — NBE)
- ኢትዮ ቴሌኮም (Ethio Telecom)
- ዩናይትድ ባንክ (United Bank)

## Numbers & Amounts
- Always write: 1,000 ብር (not 1000 ብር — use comma separator)
- Always write percentages as: 25.5% (not ፳፭.፭%)
- Use Arabic numerals not Ethiopic numerals for financial data

## Sentences Structure Rules
- End statements with ። (Ethiopic full stop)
- End questions with ? or ። 
- Use ፣ for comma pauses
- Never mix Latin punctuation randomly with Ethiopic text
